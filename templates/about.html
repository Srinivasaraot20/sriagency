📖 Our Story

Serving Rayalaseema Farmers with
Fertilizers, Seeds & Trust

Sri Ranganatha Agency is a reputed name in the agricultural sector, dedicated to supplying quality fertilisers, crop protection products and premium seeds to farmers in Nandyala and the Rayalaseema region.

We believe a successful farm starts with the right products and the right advice. With years of experience, we strive to provide reliable agricultural solutions that enhance crop health, boost productivity and ensure sustainable farming practices.

From paddy, cotton, chilli, groundnut, maize to all other crops, we provide authentic products from trusted brands to help farmers achieve better yield and healthier harvests.
